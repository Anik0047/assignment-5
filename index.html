Blankets to help poor families

Weather in Bangladesh becomes quite cold from November onwards. The temperatures drop to about 7 Degrees Celcius. Fog and rain causes many, without shoes, winter coats or cars, to be unable to go to work or school. Houses have gaps under the doors allowing cold winds to blow straight through . Adults and children sleep on soil floors rather than on beds.
